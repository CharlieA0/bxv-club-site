Welcome to Bronxville Programming.

We aim to bring advanced programmers and new coders together to build club projects, develop our skills, and have fun coding as a group.

This year we have several cool projects lined up, including a club website, a BXV App, a Varsity Sports Fantasy Game, an Investment-Club-collaboration Stock Algorithm, and an assignment posting tool. We also plan to run learning segments on Computer Security, Digital Networks, and other topics.

Anyway, come down the C-Wing Computer Lab during Lunch, Wednesday and join us for Coding, Computing, and more fun. Absolutely no experience required.
